## Introduction
Managing money is not just about earning, but about tracking and controlling expenses. Many people earn well but still struggle to save because they do not know where their money is going.

Expense management helps you understand your spending habits and improve financial stability.

---

## What is Expense Management?
Expense management is the process of tracking, categorizing, and controlling your expenses.

It includes:
- Recording daily expenses  
- Categorizing spending  
- Analyzing financial patterns  

This helps in making better financial decisions.

---

## Why Expense Management is Important

- Better financial control  
- Identify unnecessary expenses  
- Increase savings  
- Improve budget planning  
- Reduce financial stress  

Without tracking, it becomes difficult to manage money effectively.

---

## Types of Expenses

### Fixed Expenses
- Rent  
- EMI  
- Salaries  

### Variable Expenses
- Food  
- Shopping  
- Travel  

### Occasional Expenses
- Medical bills  
- Repairs  
- Festivals  

---

## How to Track Expenses Effectively

### Step 1: Record Every Expense
Write down all expenses, even small ones.

### Step 2: Categorize Spending
Divide expenses into categories like food, rent, and travel.

### Step 3: Set a Budget
Assign a limit for each category.

### Step 4: Review Regularly
Analyze weekly and monthly spending.

### Step 5: Reduce Unnecessary Expenses
Cut down on subscriptions and impulse purchases.

---

## Best Ways to Manage Expenses

### Manual Tracking
Using a notebook or diary.

### Spreadsheet Tracking
Using Excel or Google Sheets.

### Expense Tracking Apps
Automated tracking with reports and insights.
